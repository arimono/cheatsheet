## Overview
Network exposure matrix reporting organizes open services, affected assets, owners, and remediation priority.

## Steps
- Group findings by host and service.
- Identify internet-facing and unauthenticated exposure first.
- Include scan command, timestamp, and tool version.
- Map each exposure to a business owner.
- Add retest commands and expected closed or restricted state.

## Evidence
Capture host, port, protocol, service, exposure reason, and recommended firewall or service change.

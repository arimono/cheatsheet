## Overview
NTLM relay checks identify whether hosts and services accept relayed authentication because signing or channel binding is missing.

## Steps
- Confirm relay testing is explicitly authorized.
- Identify SMB signing gaps.
- Build a safe relay target list.
- Avoid coercion methods unless approved.
- Document realistic impact without unnecessary disruption.

## Evidence
Record host, service, signing state, relay target, and proof of accepted authentication.

## Overview
VPN exposure review fingerprints remote-access services and checks whether the edge login surface is hardened.

## Steps
- Identify exposed VPN portals and ports.
- Fingerprint product and version safely.
- Check certificate and banner leakage.
- Review MFA and lockout expectations.
- Escalate outdated versions to patch validation.

## Evidence
Record host, product, version signal, authentication controls, and exposure risk.

## Overview
Cloud control gap reporting summarizes identity, network, storage, and workload risks across accounts or projects.

## Steps
- Group findings by control family.
- Record account, subscription, project, and region.
- Explain blast radius and data classification.
- Map remediation to cloud-native controls.
- Provide retest commands and expected secure output.

## Evidence
Capture cloud scope, affected resource, control gap, risk owner, and remediation status.

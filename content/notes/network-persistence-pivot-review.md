## Overview
Pivot and tunnel persistence review checks whether long-lived listeners, tunnels, or firewall changes could maintain network access.

## Steps
- Confirm pivot validation is in scope.
- Inventory unexpected listeners and outbound connections.
- Correlate process, user, and start time.
- Review firewall rules and startup locations.
- Document cleanup and monitoring opportunities.

## Evidence
Record host, process, connection, port, owning user, and whether it was test-created or pre-existing.

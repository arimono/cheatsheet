## Overview
AD attack path reporting turns graph findings into clear remediation priorities for administrators and risk owners.

## Steps
- Summarize the attack path in business language.
- Include affected users, groups, computers, and privileges.
- Separate proof from sensitive credential material.
- Provide remediation by path segment.
- Add retest steps and expected secure state.

## Evidence
Include BloodHound path screenshot, impacted principal, privilege gained, and remediation owner.

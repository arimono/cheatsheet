## Overview
Network evidence reporting keeps scan output, proof, and remediation details organized for final delivery and retesting.

## Steps
- Preserve raw scan files and timestamps.
- Save minimal proof for sensitive findings.
- Separate screenshots, scans, loot, and notes.
- Write reproduction steps that a retester can follow.
- Hash important evidence files when chain of custody matters.

## Evidence
Include asset, timestamp, tool version, command, output snippet, and remediation status.

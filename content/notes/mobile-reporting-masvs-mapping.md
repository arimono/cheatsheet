## Overview
MASVS reporting maps mobile findings to OWASP controls and separates static, dynamic, and traffic evidence.

## Steps
- Record app version, package, device, and OS version.
- Map each finding to MASVS control areas.
- Separate static analysis, runtime, and proxy evidence.
- Avoid storing live secrets in the final report.
- Add platform-specific retest steps.

## Evidence
Capture MASVS control, affected file or endpoint, device context, screenshot, and remediation.

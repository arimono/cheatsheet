## Overview
GCP IAM review maps project bindings, service accounts, impersonation rights, and broad roles.

## Steps
- Confirm project and identity are in scope.
- Export project IAM policy.
- Identify owner, editor, and broad custom roles.
- Check service account impersonation rights.
- Recommend least-privilege bindings.

## Evidence
Capture principal, role, resource scope, risky permission, and remediation.

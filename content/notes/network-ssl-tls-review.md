## Overview
SSL/TLS service review checks certificates, protocol versions, cipher suites, and transport hardening across exposed services.

## Steps
- Identify TLS-enabled ports.
- Capture certificate metadata and chain issues.
- Check deprecated protocols and weak ciphers.
- Confirm hostname and expiry problems.
- Prioritize findings by exposed service and data sensitivity.

## Evidence
Record service, certificate subject, expiry, weak protocol or cipher, and remediation owner.

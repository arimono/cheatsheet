## Overview
Keystore token persistence review checks whether mobile credentials persist after logout, data clearing, or reinstall events.

## Steps
- Confirm local data review is authorized.
- Identify tokens and keys stored by the app.
- Check whether logout removes persistent credentials.
- Validate behavior after app data clear or reinstall.
- Document storage path, lifetime, and remediation.

## Evidence
Record platform, package, storage location, token type, lifecycle event, replay result, and remediation action.

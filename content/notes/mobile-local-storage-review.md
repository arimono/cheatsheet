## Overview
Local storage review checks whether mobile apps store tokens, PII, or secrets in accessible files, preferences, or databases.

## Steps
- Confirm app data extraction is allowed.
- Review shared preferences, files, and databases.
- Search for tokens, credentials, and PII.
- Check encryption and key management.
- Validate whether data remains after logout.

## Evidence
Record file path, data type, sensitivity, encryption state, and account context.

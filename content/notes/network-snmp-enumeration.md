## Overview
SNMP enumeration checks whether devices expose management data through weak or default community strings.

## Steps
- Confirm UDP probing is allowed.
- Test common community strings safely.
- Walk system, interface, route, and process data.
- Avoid write community tests unless explicitly approved.
- Use findings to prioritize network segmentation issues.

## Evidence
Record community string, OIDs queried, exposed data, and affected device.

## Overview
iOS plist and entitlement review inspects app metadata, URL schemes, keychain groups, and transport security configuration.

## Steps
- Unpack the IPA.
- Review `Info.plist` and entitlements.
- Check URL schemes and ATS exceptions.
- Inspect keychain access groups.
- Send interesting schemes to deep-link testing.

## Evidence
Capture plist key, entitlement, value, and related security impact.
